Chicken Salad With Yellow Bean Soup

This Chicken Salad with Yellow Bean Soup recipe offers a delightful balance between light, fresh flavors and warm, comforting goodness.

Ingredients

    Yellow Bean Soup

  • 1 cup dried yellow beans
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 small carrot, diced
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

    • Chicken Salad

  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast, shredded
  • 1 cup mixed salad greens
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 red onion, sliced
  • 1/4 cup bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro or parsley, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Steps

    Yellow Bean Soup

  1. Sauté: In a pot, heat olive oil. Sauté onion, garlic, carrot, and celery for 3-4 minutes.
  2. Add Beans & Broth: Add beans, turmeric, and broth. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 25-30 minutes.
  3. Season: Add cumin, salt, and pepper. Optional: Blend half for a creamier texture.

    4. Chicken Salad

  4. Prepare Dressing: Mix olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  5. Assemble Salad: Combine chicken, greens, cucumber, tomatoes, onion, bell pepper, and herbs.
  6. Toss: Drizzle with dressing and gently toss. Serve with the soup.
