Crab Masala

Crab Masala is a vibrant and flavorful South Asian dish that combines fresh crab with an aromatic blend of spices and a rich, tangy tomato-based sauce.

Ingredients

  • 500g fresh crab, cleaned and cracked
  • 2 tablespoons oil (vegetable or coconut oil)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit lengthwise
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste (optional, for tanginess)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Saut√© Base: Heat oil, add onions, and cook until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Add Tomatoes & Spices: Stir in tomatoes, green chilies, turmeric, chili powder, coriander, and cumin. Cook until oil separates.
  3. Add Crab: Add crab, coat with masala, cover, and cook for 5-10 minutes.
  4. Finish: Add garam masala, tamarind paste (optional), and salt. Simmer until thick.
  5. Garnish & Serve: Top with cilantro and serve hot with rice or flatbreads.
