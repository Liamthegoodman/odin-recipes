Starchy Gravy Noodle

Starchy Gravy Noodle is a comforting, savory dish featuring noodles coated in a thick, flavorful gravy made from broth and starch-based ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 200g noodles (e.g., egg noodles, rice noodles, or wheat noodles)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 150g protein of choice (chicken, shrimp, tofu, or beef)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, bok choy, carrots)
  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional, for extra flavor)
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch (or potato starch), mixed with 3 tablespoons water to form a slurry
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions or fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook Noodles: Prepare noodles as per package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. Sauté Aromatics: In a pan, heat oil and sauté garlic, ginger, and onion until fragrant.
  3. Add Protein & Vegetables: Add your choice of protein and cook until nearly done, then add vegetables and stir-fry.
  4. Make Gravy: Pour in broth, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Bring to a simmer.
  5. Thicken Sauce: Stir in cornstarch slurry, cooking until the gravy thickens.
  6. Combine: Add cooked noodles, toss to coat with gravy.
  7. Serve: Garnish with green onions or cilantro and enjoy hot!
